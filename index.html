Brief Biography

I'm a third-year computer science Ph.D. student at University at Buffalo (UBuffalo), SUNY, working with Prof.Siwei Lyu. I spend my first and second year at University at Albany (UAlbany), SUNY. During the first year of my Ph.D., I was working with Prof.Feng Chen.

I received my Master of Arts (Mathematics) degree from UAlbany, SUNY in 2020 under the advising of Prof.Yiming Ying and I also received my Master of Engineering degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016 under the advising of Prof.En-Hong Chen. I was previously a visiting student (2014-2015) at University of South Australia (UniSA), where I worked with Prof.Jiuyong Li and Dr.Thuc Duy Le.

My current research interests are data mining, machine learning and deep learning, especially Optimization in Machine Learning and Bayesian Deep Learning. I also interest in information security, biomedical informatics, internet of things.
